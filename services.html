Long-Term Investment

Ceramic Coating & Paint Correction

Michigan winters are uniquely harsh on automotive paint — road salt, sand, freeze-thaw cycles, and summer UV destroy unprotected clear coat faster than almost anywhere else in the US. A professional ceramic coating is the definitive solution.

This service includes a full paint correction to remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation before the ceramic is applied — ensuring a flawless base that makes the coating last 2–5 years. Every ceramic client in Commerce Township, Bloomfield Hills, and West Bloomfield gets JP's personal attention for an entire day.

  • Full Signature Package preparation
  • Multi-stage paint decontamination
  • Iron & fallout removal
  • Paint correction — swirls, scratches, oxidation
  • Professional ceramic coating applied
  • Hydrophobic glass coating
  • Wheel & caliper ceramic coating
  • Aftercare kit included
  • 1–2 year protection warranty

What You Need to Have at Your Location

Access to a working outdoor water source (garden hose spigot) and a standard 110V electrical outlet is required at the service location. This applies to all detailing services — from a basic exterior wash to the full Signature Package and ceramic coating. Most homes in Commerce Township, West Bloomfield, Walled Lake, and surrounding Oakland County communities have both readily available. Apartments and condos may have shared spigots — give us a call and we'll confirm availability before scheduling.
